MUSIC SETUP
===========

Add your two music files to this folder:

  assets/music-under-the-sea.mp3     (Track 1: "Under the Sea" - Little Mermaid)
  assets/music-bubble-guppies.mp3    (Track 2: "Bubble Guppies Theme")

Once you've previewed both and picked one:
  Open index.html and delete the <audio> element you DON'T want.
  Each <audio> tag has a comment above it marking which one to remove.

Sources:
  - YouTube to MP3 converters (for personal use only)
  - Or purchase from iTunes / Amazon Music
